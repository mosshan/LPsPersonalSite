MY HORSE RIDING CAREER

September 7, 2018

A little insight on my life, I have been riding in competitive horse jumping since a very young age. My horse and I now train at the international level, which qualified us for the U21 North American Junior and Young Rider Championships. Together, we strive for the Young Riders team of four riders. I have competed in multiple international and national championships placing in the upper half of 100 competitors. Together, my horse and I continue to compete at the upper levels of our discipline called eventing.
