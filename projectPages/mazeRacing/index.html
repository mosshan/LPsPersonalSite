ROBOT MAZE RACING

with Trevor Bernstein, Franco Curcio, and Noah Jackson

Robot Maze Racing was a project created for a UCF engineering competition that raced random and unique mazes. Our team of four would receive the course design in less than 5 minutes before running then proceed to code in C language the turns and bumper sensors. Overall, our team placed 3rd in a competition of 350 teams.
