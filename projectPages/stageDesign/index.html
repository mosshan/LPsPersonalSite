STAGE DESIGN

My stagecraft class had been building the set for the play Who is Theo?. We have been instructed by a set designer for the dimensions that you can see proportional to the person in the second photo. My group and I built the flat panels on the floor. Then I went up on the ladder to screw in the top window panels, doors, and top panel to secure the walls. The progress is still continuing.
