INTERACTIVE CHESS BOARD

The Interactive Chess Board is a game that plays chess. It features a 3D printed robotic arm that reaches down with a solenoid and utilizes the magnetic field of the solenoid coil to transfer the chess piece around the board. The chess pieces contain ferromagnetic material at the core - iron fillings- which responds to the magnetism. The magnetic field opens a new side to this project, while creating a release mechanism. The project is at the preliminary stages with multiple options to take in the future. Whether it be a coded robotic arm to decide its own moves with AI abilities or a panel control of the robotic arm. The continuation of the project will transfer into the next modules for an innovative experience.
