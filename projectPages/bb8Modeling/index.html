MODELING BB8 MECHANISM

with Trevor Bernstein

Currently, my partner and I are designing the BB8 Model from Star Wars. These are the beginning steps of 3D modeling, so the approach for building this structure is visually clear. There are some new aspects added in our model such as a joystick mechanism for the head motion and different shapes of the four wheels in the inner circle. We are continuing to build this model!
