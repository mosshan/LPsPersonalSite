A MATTER OF HUSBANDS STAGE DESIGN

with Chase Trinkein and Tyler Kelton

I had to opportunity to create a set design for the play A Matter Of Husbands during my Stagecraft class. My group and I created our idea, which we laser cut our designs to the perfect 1/4 scale to the real set. There were the incorporation of shelves, multiple doors, tables, and chairs. This was the first step in designing a theater set design.
