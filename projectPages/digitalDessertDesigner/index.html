DIGITAL DESSERT DESIGNER

with Walter Mattson

Digital Dessert Designer is a tool that allows chefs to print complex designs and patterns on cakes, cookies, and other decoratable foods. This culinary 3d printer has an extruder attached to a servo motor, which moves in a radial motion. The user of the DDD programs the speed of the extruderâ€™s movement and controls the rate of extrusion. Together, my partner and I built an innovative culinary experience.
