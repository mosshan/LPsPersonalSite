BLANKET CLOTHES

with Cambria Gosh

Blanket Clothes was thought of making a wooden structure to go under the fabric, so the clothes were transformable. I came up with a design to focus on clothes that have multiple functions. Together, my partner and I tried to use multiple materials that were both warm and cold. The double sided material helped keep in warmth, while releasing the sweat. The clothes were on the beginning steps starting at the sleeves to continue to a full jacket.
